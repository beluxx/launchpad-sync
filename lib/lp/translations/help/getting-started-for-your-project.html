Getting started with Launchpad Translations for your project

Launchpad lets you open your project to translation by a community of thousands.

Before you start using Launchpad to translate your project, you need to activate translations for your project and then:

  • join a translations group, who will help you review your project's translations — Read about translation groups >
  • select a translation permissions policy — Read about permissions policies >
  • format then import your project's translation templates and any existing translations — Read how to format and import your templates >

Important: you also need to learn the GNU GetText method of integrating translations in your software. Read the GNU GetText manual >
