Reviewing translations

Important: You can find the translation guidelines you must follow as a reviewer by visiting the appropriate translation group from the translation group overview page.

As a member of one or more translation teams, you can help make sure that that the work of other translators makes it into published software.

One of the quickest ways is to review translated strings in the languages with which you're familiar. The more reviews you do, the more software will be available in the languages you care about.

To get started, simply follow the review links on your translation dashboard.

Note: there's a lot more to being a member of a translation team than just reviewing strings. Most translation teams have an active community that works to set standards and divide up work among its members.
